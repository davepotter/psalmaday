<h3>Psalm 89</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A contemplation by Ethan, the Ezrahite.] I will sing of the loving kindness of Yahweh forever. With my mouth, I will make known your faithfulness to all generations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> I indeed declare, "Love stands firm forever. You established the heavens. Your faithfulness is in them."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> "I have made a covenant with my chosen one, I have sworn to David, my servant,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> 'I will establish your seed forever, and build up your throne to all generations.'" Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> The heavens will praise your wonders, Yahweh; your faithfulness also in the assembly of the holy ones.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> For who in the skies can be compared to Yahweh? Who among the sons of the heavenly beings is like Yahweh,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> a very awesome God in the council of the holy ones, to be feared above all those who are around him?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, God of Armies, who is a mighty one, like you? Yah, your faithfulness is around you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> You rule the pride of the sea. When its waves rise up, you calm them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> You have broken Rahab in pieces, like one of the slain. You have scattered your enemies with your mighty arm.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> The heavens are yours. The earth also is yours; the world and its fullness. You have founded them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> The north and the south, you have created them. Tabor and Hermon rejoice in your name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> You have a mighty arm. Your hand is strong, and your right hand is exalted.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Righteousness and justice are the foundation of your throne. Loving kindness and truth go before your face.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Blessed are the people who learn to acclaim you. They walk in the light of your presence, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> In your name they rejoice all day. In your righteousness, they are exalted.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> For you are the glory of their strength. In your favor, our horn will be exalted.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> For our shield belongs to Yahweh; our king to the Holy One of Israel.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Then you spoke in vision to your saints, and said, "I have bestowed strength on the warrior. I have exalted a young man from the people.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> I have found David, my servant. I have anointed him with my holy oil,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> with whom my hand shall be established. My arm will also strengthen him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> No enemy will tax him. No wicked man will oppress him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> I will beat down his adversaries before him, and strike those who hate him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> But my faithfulness and my loving kindness will be with him. In my name, his horn will be exalted.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> I will set his hand also on the sea, and his right hand on the rivers.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> He will call to me, 'You are my Father, my God, and the rock of my salvation!'</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">27</div><div class="verse"> I will also appoint him my firstborn, the highest of the kings of the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">28</div><div class="verse"> I will keep my loving kindness for him forevermore. My covenant will stand firm with him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">29</div><div class="verse"> I will also make his seed endure forever, and his throne as the days of heaven.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">30</div><div class="verse"> If his children forsake my law, and don't walk in my ordinances;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">31</div><div class="verse"> if they break my statutes, and don't keep my commandments;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">32</div><div class="verse"> then I will punish their sin with the rod, and their iniquity with stripes.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">33</div><div class="verse"> But I will not completely take my loving kindness from him, nor allow my faithfulness to fail.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">34</div><div class="verse"> I will not break my covenant, nor alter what my lips have uttered.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">35</div><div class="verse"> Once have I sworn by my holiness, I will not lie to David.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">36</div><div class="verse"> His seed will endure forever, his throne like the sun before me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">37</div><div class="verse"> It will be established forever like the moon, the faithful witness in the sky." Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">38</div><div class="verse"> But you have rejected and spurned. You have been angry with your anointed.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">39</div><div class="verse"> You have renounced the covenant of your servant. You have defiled his crown in the dust.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">40</div><div class="verse"> You have broken down all his hedges. You have brought his strongholds to ruin.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">41</div><div class="verse"> All who pass by the way rob him. He has become a reproach to his neighbors.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">42</div><div class="verse"> You have exalted the right hand of his adversaries. You have made all of his enemies rejoice.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">43</div><div class="verse"> Yes, you turn back the edge of his sword, and haven't supported him in battle.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">44</div><div class="verse"> You have ended his splendor, and thrown his throne down to the ground.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">45</div><div class="verse"> You have shortened the days of his youth. You have covered him with shame. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">46</div><div class="verse"> How long, Yahweh? Will you hide yourself forever? Will your wrath burn like fire?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">47</div><div class="verse"> Remember how short my time is! For what vanity have you created all the children of men!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">48</div><div class="verse"> What man is he who shall live and not see death, who shall deliver his soul from the power of Sheol? Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">49</div><div class="verse"> Lord, where are your former loving kindnesses, which you swore to David in your faithfulness?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">50</div><div class="verse"> Remember, Lord, the reproach of your servants, how I bear in my heart the taunts of all the mighty peoples,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">51</div><div class="verse"> With which your enemies have mocked, Yahweh, with which they have mocked the footsteps of your anointed one.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">52</div><div class="verse"> Blessed be Yahweh forevermore. Amen, and Amen.</div>
</div>
</div>
