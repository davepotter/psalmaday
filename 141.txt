<h3>Psalm 141</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm by David.] Yahweh, I have called on you. Come to me quickly! Listen to my voice when I call to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Let my prayer be set before you like incense; the lifting up of my hands like the evening sacrifice.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Set a watch, Yahweh, before my mouth. Keep the door of my lips.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Don't incline my heart to any evil thing, to practice deeds of wickedness with men who work iniquity. Don't let me eat of their delicacies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Let the righteous strike me, it is kindness; let him reprove me, it is like oil on the head; don't let my head refuse it; Yet my prayer is always against evil deeds.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Their judges are thrown down by the sides of the rock. They will hear my words, for they are well spoken.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> "As when one plows and breaks up the earth, our bones are scattered at the mouth of Sheol."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> For my eyes are on you, Yahweh, the Lord. In you, I take refuge. Don't leave my soul destitute.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Keep me from the snare which they have laid for me, from the traps of the workers of iniquity.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Let the wicked fall together into their own nets, while I pass by.</div>
</div>
</div>
