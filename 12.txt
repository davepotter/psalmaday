<h3>Psalm 12</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician; upon an eight-stringed lyre. A Psalm of David.] Help, Yahweh; for the godly man ceases. For the faithful fail from among the children of men.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Everyone lies to his neighbor. They speak with flattering lips, and with a double heart.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> May Yahweh cut off all flattering lips, and the tongue that boasts,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> who have said, "With our tongue we will prevail. Our lips are our own. Who is lord over us?"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> "Because of the oppression of the weak and because of the groaning of the needy, I will now arise," says Yahweh; "I will set him in safety from those who malign him."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> The words of Yahweh are flawless words, as silver refined in a clay furnace, purified seven times.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> You will keep them, Yahweh. You will preserve them from this generation forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> The wicked walk on every side, when what is vile is exalted among the sons of men.</div>
</div>
</div>
