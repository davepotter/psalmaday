<h3>Psalm 98</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm.] Sing to Yahweh a new song, for he has done marvelous things! His right hand, and his holy arm, have worked salvation for him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh has made known his salvation. He has openly shown his righteousness in the sight of the nations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> He has remembered his loving kindness and his faithfulness toward the house of Israel. All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Make a joyful noise to Yahweh, all the earth! Burst out and sing for joy, yes, sing praises!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Sing praises to Yahweh with the harp, with the harp and the voice of melody.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> With trumpets and sound of the ram's horn, make a joyful noise before the King, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Let the sea roar with its fullness; the world, and those who dwell therein.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Let the rivers clap their hands. Let the mountains sing for joy together.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Let them sing before Yahweh, for he comes to judge the earth. He will judge the world with righteousness, and the peoples with equity.</div>
</div>
</div>
