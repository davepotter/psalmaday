<h3>Psalm 79</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm by Asaph.] God, the nations have come into your inheritance. They have defiled your holy temple. They have laid Jerusalem in heaps.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> They have given the dead bodies of your servants to be food for the birds of the sky, the flesh of your saints to the animals of the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Their blood they have shed like water around Jerusalem. There was no one to bury them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> We have become a reproach to our neighbors, a scoffing and derision to those who are around us.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> How long, Yahweh? Will you be angry forever? Will your jealousy burn like fire?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Pour out your wrath on the nations that don't know you; on the kingdoms that don't call on your name;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> For they have devoured Jacob, and destroyed his homeland.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Don't hold the iniquities of our forefathers against us. Let your tender mercies speedily meet us, for we are in desperate need.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Help us, God of our salvation, for the glory of your name. Deliver us, and forgive our sins, for your name's sake.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Why should the nations say, "Where is their God?" Let it be known among the nations, before our eyes, that vengeance for your servants' blood is being poured out.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Let the sighing of the prisoner come before you. According to the greatness of your power, preserve those who are sentenced to death.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Pay back to our neighbors seven times into their bosom their reproach with which they have reproached you, Lord.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> So we, your people and sheep of your pasture, will give you thanks forever. We will praise you forever, to all generations.</div>
</div>
</div>
