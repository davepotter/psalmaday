<h3>Psalm 26</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David.] Judge me, Yahweh, for I have walked in my integrity. I have trusted also in Yahweh without wavering.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Examine me, Yahweh, and prove me. Try my heart and my mind.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For your loving kindness is before my eyes. I have walked in your truth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> I have not sat with deceitful men, neither will I go in with hypocrites.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> I hate the assembly of evildoers, and will not sit with the wicked.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> I will wash my hands in innocence, so I will go about your altar, Yahweh;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> that I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard, and tell of all your wondrous works.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, I love the habitation of your house, the place where your glory dwells.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Don't gather my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloodthirsty men;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> in whose hands is wickedness, their right hand is full of bribes.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> But as for me, I will walk in my integrity. Redeem me, and be merciful to me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> My foot stands in an even place. In the congregations I will bless Yahweh.</div>
</div>
</div>
