<h3>Psalm 34</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David; when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, who drove him away, and he departed.Psalm 34 is an acrostic poem, with each verse starting with a letter of the alphabet (ordered from Alef to Tav).] I will bless Yahweh at all times. His praise will always be in my mouth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> My soul shall boast in Yahweh. The humble shall hear of it, and be glad.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Oh magnify Yahweh with me. Let us exalt his name together.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> I sought Yahweh, and he answered me, and delivered me from all my fears.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> They looked to him, and were radiant. Their faces shall never be covered with shame.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> This poor man cried, and Yahweh heard him, and saved him out of all his troubles.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> The angel of Yahweh encamps around those who fear him, and delivers them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Oh taste and see that Yahweh is good. Blessed is the man who takes refuge in him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Oh fear Yahweh, you his saints, for there is no lack with those who fear him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger, but those who seek Yahweh shall not lack any good thing.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Come, you children, listen to me. I will teach you the fear of Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Who is someone who desires life, and loves many days, that he may see good?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Keep your tongue from evil, and your lips from speaking lies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Depart from evil, and do good. seek peace, and pursue it.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh's eyes are toward the righteous. His ears listen to their cry.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh's face is against those who do evil, to cut off the memory of them from the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> The righteous cry, and Yahweh hears, and delivers them out of all their troubles.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is near to those who have a broken heart, and saves those who have a crushed spirit.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Many are the afflictions of the righteous, but Yahweh delivers him out of them all.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> He protects all of his bones. Not one of them is broken.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Evil shall kill the wicked. Those who hate the righteous shall be condemned.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh redeems the soul of his servants. None of those who take refuge in him shall be condemned.</div>
</div>
</div>
