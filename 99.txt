<h3>Psalm 99</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh reigns! Let the peoples tremble. He sits enthroned among the cherubim. Let the earth be moved.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is great in Zion. He is high above all the peoples.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise your great and awesome name. He is Holy!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> The King's strength also loves justice. You do establish equity. You execute justice and righteousness in Jacob.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Exalt Yahweh our God. Worship at his footstool. He is Holy!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Moses and Aaron were among his priests, Samuel among those who call on his name; they called on Yahweh, and he answered them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> He spoke to them in the pillar of cloud. They kept his testimonies, the statute that he gave them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> You answered them, Yahweh our God. You are a God who forgave them, although you took vengeance for their doings.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Exalt Yahweh, our God. Worship at his holy hill, for Yahweh, our God, is holy!</div>
</div>
</div>
