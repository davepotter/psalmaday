<h3>Psalm 44</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. By the sons of Korah. A contemplative psalm.] We have heard with our ears, God; our fathers have told us, what work you did in their days, in the days of old.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> You drove out the nations with your hand, but you planted them. You afflicted the peoples, but you spread them abroad.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For they didn't get the land in possession by their own sword, neither did their own arm save them; but your right hand, and your arm, and the light of your face, because you were favorable to them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> You are my King, God. Command victories for Jacob!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Through you, will we push down our adversaries. Through your name, will we tread them under who rise up against us.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> For I will not trust in my bow, neither shall my sword save me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> But you have saved us from our adversaries, and have shamed those who hate us.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> In God we have made our boast all day long, we will give thanks to your name forever. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> But now you rejected us, and brought us to dishonor, and don't go out with our armies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> You make us turn back from the adversary. Those who hate us take spoil for themselves.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> You have made us like sheep for food, and have scattered us among the nations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> You sell your people for nothing, and have gained nothing from their sale.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> You make us a reproach to our neighbors, a scoffing and a derision to those who are around us.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> You make us a byword among the nations, a shaking of the head among the peoples.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> All day long my dishonor is before me, and shame covers my face,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> At the taunt of one who reproaches and verbally abuses, because of the enemy and the avenger.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> All this has come on us, yet have we not forgotten you, Neither have we been false to your covenant.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Our heart has not turned back, neither have our steps strayed from your path,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Though you have crushed us in the haunt of jackals, and covered us with the shadow of death.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> If we have forgotten the name of our God, or spread forth our hands to a strange god;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> won't God search this out? For he knows the secrets of the heart.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Yes, for your sake we are killed all day long. We are regarded as sheep for the slaughter.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Wake up! Why do you sleep, Lord? Arise! Don't reject us forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> Why do you hide your face, and forget our affliction and our oppression?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> For our soul is bowed down to the dust. Our body cleaves to the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> Rise up to help us. Redeem us for your loving kindness' sake.</div>
</div>
</div>
