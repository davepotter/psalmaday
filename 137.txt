<h3>Psalm 137</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> By the rivers of Babylon, there we sat down. Yes, we wept, when we remembered Zion.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> On the willows in its midst, we hung up our harps.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For there, those who led us captive asked us for songs. Those who tormented us demanded songs of joy: "Sing us one of the songs of Zion!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> How can we sing Yahweh's song in a foreign land?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> If I forget you, Jerusalem, let my right hand forget its skill.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Let my tongue stick to the roof of my mouth if I don't remember you; if I don't prefer Jerusalem above my chief joy.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Remember, Yahweh, against the children of Edom, the day of Jerusalem; who said, "Raze it! Raze it even to its foundation!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Daughter of Babylon, doomed to destruction, he will be happy who rewards you, as you have served us.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Happy shall he be, who takes and dashes your little ones against the rock.</div>
</div>
</div>
