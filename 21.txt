<h3>Psalm 21</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David.] The king rejoices in your strength, Yahweh! How greatly he rejoices in your salvation!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> You have given him his heart's desire, and have not withheld the request of his lips. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For you meet him with the blessings of goodness. You set a crown of fine gold on his head.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> He asked life of you, you gave it to him, even length of days forever and ever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> His glory is great in your salvation. You lay honor and majesty on him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> For you make him most blessed forever. You make him glad with joy in your presence.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> For the king trusts in Yahweh. Through the loving kindness of the Most High, he shall not be moved.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Your hand will find out all of your enemies. Your right hand will find out those who hate you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> You will make them as a fiery furnace in the time of your anger. Yahweh will swallow them up in his wrath. The fire shall devour them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> You will destroy their descendants from the earth, their posterity from among the children of men.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> For they intended evil against you. They plotted evil against you which cannot succeed.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> For you will make them turn their back, when you aim drawn bows at their face.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Be exalted, Yahweh, in your strength, so we will sing and praise your power.</div>
</div>
</div>
