<h3>Psalm 61</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. For a stringed instrument. By David.] Hear my cry, God. Listen to my prayer.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> From the end of the earth, I will call to you, when my heart is overwhelmed. Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For you have been a refuge for me, a strong tower from the enemy.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> I will dwell in your tent forever. I will take refuge in the shelter of your wings. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For you, God, have heard my vows. You have given me the heritage of those who fear your name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> You will prolong the king's life; his years shall be for generations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> He shall be enthroned in God's presence forever. Appoint your loving kindness and truth, that they may preserve him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> So I will sing praise to your name forever, that I may fulfill my vows daily.</div>
</div>
</div>
