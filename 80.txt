<h3>Psalm 80</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. To the tune of "The Lilies of the Covenant." A Psalm by Asaph.] Hear us, Shepherd of Israel, you who lead Joseph like a flock, you who sit above the cherubim, shine forth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh, stir up your might! Come to save us!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Turn us again, God. Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh God of Armies, How long will you be angry against the prayer of your people?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> You have fed them with the bread of tears, and given them tears to drink in large measure.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> You make us a source of contention to our neighbors. Our enemies laugh among themselves.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Turn us again, God of Armies. Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> You brought a vine out of Egypt. You drove out the nations, and planted it.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> You cleared the ground for it. It took deep root, and filled the land.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> The mountains were covered with its shadow. Its boughs were like God's cedars.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> It sent out its branches to the sea, Its shoots to the River.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Why have you broken down its walls, so that all those who pass by the way pluck it?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> The boar out of the wood ravages it. The wild animals of the field feed on it.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Turn again, we beg you, God of Armies. Look down from heaven, and see, and visit this vine,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> the stock which your right hand planted, the branch that you made strong for yourself.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> It's burned with fire. It's cut down. They perish at your rebuke.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Let your hand be on the man of your right hand, on the son of man whom you made strong for yourself.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> So we will not turn away from you. Revive us, and we will call on your name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Turn us again, Yahweh God of Armies. Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.</div>
</div>
</div>
