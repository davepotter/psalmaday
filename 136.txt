<h3>Psalm 136</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good; for his loving kindness endures forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Give thanks to the God of gods; for his loving kindness endures forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Give thanks to the Lord of lords; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> To him who alone does great wonders; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> To him who by understanding made the heavens; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> To him who spread out the earth above the waters; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> To him who made the great lights; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> The sun to rule by day; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> The moon and stars to rule by night; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> To him who struck down the Egyptian firstborn; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> And brought out Israel from among them; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> With a strong hand, and with an outstretched arm; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> To him who divided the Red Sea{or, Sea of Reeds} apart; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> And made Israel to pass through its midst; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> But overthrew Pharaoh and his army in the Red Sea{or, Sea of Reeds}; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> To him who led his people through the wilderness; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> To him who struck great kings; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> And killed mighty kings; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Sihon king of the Amorites; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> Og king of Bashan; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> And gave their land as an inheritance; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Even a heritage to Israel his servant; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Who remembered us in our low estate; for his loving kindness endures forever;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> And has delivered us from our adversaries; for his loving kindness endures forever:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> Who gives food to every creature; for his loving kindness endures forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> Oh give thanks to the God of heaven; for his loving kindness endures forever.</div>
</div>
</div>
