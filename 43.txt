<h3>Psalm 43</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Vindicate me, God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation. Oh, deliver me from deceitful and wicked men.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> For you are the God of my strength. Why have you rejected me? Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Oh, send out your light and your truth. Let them lead me. Let them bring me to your holy hill, To your tents.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Then I will go to the altar of God, to God, my exceeding joy. I will praise you on the harp, God, my God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Why are you in despair, my soul? Why are you disturbed within me? Hope in God! For I shall still praise him: my Savior, my helper, and my God.</div>
</div>
</div>
