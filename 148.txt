<h3>Psalm 148</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yah! Praise Yahweh from the heavens! Praise him in the heights!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Praise him, all his angels! Praise him, all his army!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Praise him, sun and moon! Praise him, all you shining stars!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Praise him, you heavens of heavens, You waters that are above the heavens.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise the name of Yahweh, For he commanded, and they were created.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> He has also established them forever and ever. He has made a decree which will not pass away.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yahweh from the earth, you great sea creatures, and all depths!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Lightning and hail, snow and clouds; stormy wind, fulfilling his word;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> mountains and all hills; fruit trees and all cedars;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> wild animals and all livestock; small creatures and flying birds;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> kings of the earth and all peoples; princes and all judges of the earth;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> both young men and maidens; old men and children:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> let them praise the name of Yahweh, for his name alone is exalted. His glory is above the earth and the heavens.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> He has lifted up the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; even of the children of Israel, a people near to him. Praise Yah!</div>
</div>
</div>
