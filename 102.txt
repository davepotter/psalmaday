<h3>Psalm 102</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Prayer of the afflicted, when he is overwhelmed and pours out his complaint before Yahweh.] Hear my prayer, Yahweh! Let my cry come to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Don't hide your face from me in the day of my distress. Turn your ear to me. Answer me quickly in the day when I call.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For my days consume away like smoke. My bones are burned as a firebrand.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> My heart is blighted like grass, and withered, for I forget to eat my bread.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> By reason of the voice of my groaning, my bones stick to my skin.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> I am like a pelican of the wilderness. I have become as an owl of the waste places.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> I watch, and have become like a sparrow that is alone on the housetop.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> My enemies reproach me all day. Those who are mad at me use my name as a curse.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> For I have eaten ashes like bread, and mixed my drink with tears,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Because of your indignation and your wrath, for you have taken me up, and thrown me away.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> My days are like a long shadow. I have withered like grass.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> But you, Yahweh, will abide forever; your renown endures to all generations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> You will arise and have mercy on Zion; for it is time to have pity on her. Yes, the set time has come.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> For your servants take pleasure in her stones, and have pity on her dust.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> So the nations will fear the name of Yahweh; all the kings of the earth your glory.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> For Yahweh has built up Zion. He has appeared in his glory.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> He has responded to the prayer of the destitute, and has not despised their prayer.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> This will be written for the generation to come. A people which will be created will praise Yah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> For he has looked down from the height of his sanctuary. From heaven, Yahweh saw the earth;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> to hear the groans of the prisoner; to free those who are condemned to death;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> that men may declare the name of Yahweh in Zion, and his praise in Jerusalem;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> when the peoples are gathered together, the kingdoms, to serve Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> He weakened my strength along the course. He shortened my days.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> I said, "My God, don't take me away in the midst of my days. Your years are throughout all generations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> Of old, you laid the foundation of the earth. The heavens are the work of your hands.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> They will perish, but you will endure. Yes, all of them will wear out like a garment. You will change them like a cloak, and they will be changed.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">27</div><div class="verse"> But you are the same. Your years will have no end.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">28</div><div class="verse"> The children of your servants will continue. Their seed will be established before you."</div>
</div>
</div>
