<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yah! Praise, you servants of Yahweh, praise the name of Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Blessed be the name of Yahweh, from this time forth and forevermore.</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is high above all nations, his glory above the heavens.</div>
</div>

<div class="row">

</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> He raises up the poor out of the dust. Lifts up the needy from the ash heap;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> that he may set him with princes, even with the princes of his people.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> He settles the barren woman in her home, as a joyful mother of children. Praise Yah!</div>
</div>
