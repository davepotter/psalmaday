<h3>Psalm 149</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yahweh! Sing to Yahweh a new song, his praise in the assembly of the saints.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Let Israel rejoice in him who made them. Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise his name in the dance! Let them sing praises to him with tambourine and harp!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> For Yahweh takes pleasure in his people. He crowns the humble with salvation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Let the saints rejoice in honor. Let them sing for joy on their beds.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> May the high praises of God be in their mouths, and a two-edged sword in their hand;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> To execute vengeance on the nations, and punishments on the peoples;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> to execute on them the written judgment. All his saints have this honor. Praise Yah!</div>
</div>
</div>
