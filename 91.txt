<h3>Psalm 91</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> He who dwells in the secret place of the Most High will rest in the shadow of the Almighty.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> I will say of Yahweh, "He is my refuge and my fortress; my God, in whom I trust."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For he will deliver you from the snare of the fowler, and from the deadly pestilence.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> He will cover you with his feathers. Under his wings you will take refuge. His faithfulness is your shield and rampart.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> You shall not be afraid of the terror by night, nor of the arrow that flies by day;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> nor of the pestilence that walks in darkness, nor of the destruction that wastes at noonday.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> A thousand may fall at your side, and ten thousand at your right hand; but it will not come near you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> You will only look with your eyes, and see the recompense of the wicked.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Because you have made Yahweh your refuge, and the Most High your dwelling place,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> no evil shall happen to you, neither shall any plague come near your dwelling.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> For he will put his angels in charge of you, to guard you in all your ways.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> They will bear you up in their hands, so that you won't dash your foot against a stone.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> You will tread on the lion and cobra. You will trample the young lion and the serpent underfoot.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> "Because he has set his love on me, therefore I will deliver him. I will set him on high, because he has known my name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> He will call on me, and I will answer him. I will be with him in trouble. I will deliver him, and honor him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> I will satisfy him with long life, and show him my salvation."</div>
</div>
</div>
