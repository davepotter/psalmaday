<h3>Psalm 62</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. To Jeduthan. A Psalm by David.] My soul rests in God alone. My salvation is from him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress-- I will never be greatly shaken.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> How long will you assault a man, would all of you throw him down, Like a leaning wall, like a tottering fence?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> They fully intend to throw him down from his lofty place. They delight in lies. They bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> My soul, wait in silence for God alone, for my expectation is from him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress. I will not be shaken.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> With God is my salvation and my honor. The rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Trust in him at all times, you people. Pour out your heart before him. God is a refuge for us. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Surely men of low degree are just a breath, and men of high degree are a lie. In the balances they will go up. They are together lighter than a breath.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Don't trust in oppression. Don't become vain in robbery. If riches increase, don't set your heart on them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> God has spoken once; twice I have heard this, that power belongs to God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Also to you, Lord, belongs loving kindness, for you reward every man according to his work.</div>
</div>
</div>
