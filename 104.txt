<h3>Psalm 104</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Bless Yahweh, my soul. Yahweh, my God, you are very great. You are clothed with honor and majesty.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> He covers himself with light as with a garment. He stretches out the heavens like a curtain.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> He lays the beams of his chambers in the waters. He makes the clouds his chariot. He walks on the wings of the wind.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> He makes his messengers{or, angels} winds; his servants flames of fire.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> He laid the foundations of the earth, that it should not be moved forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> You covered it with the deep as with a cloak. The waters stood above the mountains.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> At your rebuke they fled. At the voice of your thunder they hurried away.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> The mountains rose, the valleys sank down, to the place which you had assigned to them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> You have set a boundary that they may not pass over; that they don't turn again to cover the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> He sends forth springs into the valleys. They run among the mountains.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> They give drink to every animal of the field. The wild donkeys quench their thirst.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> The birds of the sky nest by them. They sing among the branches.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> He waters the mountains from his chambers. The earth is filled with the fruit of your works.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> He causes the grass to grow for the livestock, and plants for man to cultivate, that he may bring forth food out of the earth:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> wine that makes glad the heart of man, oil to make his face to shine, and bread that strengthens man's heart.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh's trees are well watered, the cedars of Lebanon, which he has planted;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> where the birds make their nests. The stork makes its home in the fir trees.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> The high mountains are for the wild goats. The rocks are a refuge for the rock badgers.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> He appointed the moon for seasons. The sun knows when to set.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> You make darkness, and it is night, in which all the animals of the forest prowl.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> The young lions roar after their prey, and seek their food from God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> The sun rises, and they steal away, and lay down in their dens.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Man goes forth to his work, to his labor until the evening.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, how many are your works! In wisdom have you made them all. The earth is full of your riches.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> There is the sea, great and wide, in which are innumerable living things, both small and large animals.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> There the ships go, and leviathan, whom you formed to play there.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">27</div><div class="verse"> These all wait for you, that you may give them their food in due season.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">28</div><div class="verse"> You give to them; they gather. You open your hand; they are satisfied with good.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">29</div><div class="verse"> You hide your face: they are troubled; you take away their breath: they die, and return to the dust.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">30</div><div class="verse"> You send forth your Spirit: they are created. You renew the face of the ground.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">31</div><div class="verse"> Let the glory of Yahweh endure forever. Let Yahweh rejoice in his works.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">32</div><div class="verse"> He looks at the earth, and it trembles. He touches the mountains, and they smoke.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">33</div><div class="verse"> I will sing to Yahweh as long as I live. I will sing praise to my God while I have any being.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">34</div><div class="verse"> Let your meditation be sweet to him. I will rejoice in Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">35</div><div class="verse"> Let sinners be consumed out of the earth. Let the wicked be no more. Bless Yahweh, my soul. Praise Yah!</div>
</div>
</div>
