<h3>Psalm 31</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David.] In you, Yahweh, I take refuge. Let me never be disappointed. Deliver me in your righteousness.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Bow down your ear to me. Deliver me speedily. Be to me a strong rock, a house of defense to save me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For you are my rock and my fortress, therefore for your name's sake lead me and guide me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Pluck me out of the net that they have laid secretly for me, for you are my stronghold.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Into your hand I commend my spirit. You redeem me, Yahweh, God of truth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> I hate those who regard lying vanities, but I trust in Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> I will be glad and rejoice in your loving kindness, for you have seen my affliction. You have known my soul in adversities.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> You have not shut me up into the hand of the enemy. You have set my feet in a large place.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Have mercy on me, Yahweh, for I am in distress. My eye, my soul, and my body waste away with grief.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> For my life is spent with sorrow, my years with sighing. My strength fails because of my iniquity. My bones are wasted away.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Because of all my adversaries I have become utterly contemptible to my neighbors, A fear to my acquaintances. Those who saw me on the street fled from me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> I am forgotten from their hearts like a dead man. I am like broken pottery.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> For I have heard the slander of many, terror on every side, while they conspire together against me, they plot to take away my life.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> But I trust in you, Yahweh. I said, "You are my God."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> My times are in your hand. Deliver me from the hand of my enemies, and from those who persecute me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Make your face to shine on your servant. Save me in your loving kindness.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Let me not be disappointed, Yahweh, for I have called on you. Let the wicked be disappointed. Let them be silent in Sheol.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Let the lying lips be mute, which speak against the righteous insolently, with pride and contempt.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Oh how great is your goodness, which you have laid up for those who fear you, which you have worked for those who take refuge in you, before the sons of men!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> In the shelter of your presence you will hide them from the plotting of man. You will keep them secretly in a dwelling away from the strife of tongues.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Praise be to Yahweh, for he has shown me his marvelous loving kindness in a strong city.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> As for me, I said in my haste, "I am cut off from before your eyes." Nevertheless you heard the voice of my petitions when I cried to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Oh love Yahweh, all you his saints! Yahweh preserves the faithful, and fully recompenses him who behaves arrogantly.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> Be strong, and let your heart take courage, all you who hope in Yahweh.</div>
</div>
</div>
