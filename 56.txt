<h3>Psalm 56</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. To the tune of "Silent Dove in Distant Lands." A poem by David, when the Philistines seized him in Gath.] Be merciful to me, God, for man wants to swallow me up. All day long, he attacks and oppresses me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> My enemies want to swallow me up all day long, for they are many who fight proudly against me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> When I am afraid, I will put my trust in you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> In God, I praise his word. In God, I put my trust. I will not be afraid. What can flesh do to me?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> All day long they twist my words. All their thoughts are against me for evil.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> They conspire and lurk, watching my steps, they are eager to take my life.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Shall they escape by iniquity? In anger cast down the peoples, God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> You number my wanderings. You put my tears into your bottle. Aren't they in your book?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Then my enemies shall turn back in the day that I call. I know this, that God is for me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> In God, I will praise his word. In Yahweh, I will praise his word.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> I have put my trust in God. I will not be afraid. What can man do to me?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Your vows are on me, God. I will give thank offerings to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> For you have delivered my soul from death, and prevented my feet from falling, that I may walk before God in the light of the living.</div>
</div>
</div>
