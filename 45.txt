<h3>Psalm 45</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. Set to "The Lilies." A contemplation by the sons of Korah. A wedding song.] My heart overflows with a noble theme. I recite my verses for the king. My tongue is like the pen of a skillful writer.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> You are the most excellent of the sons of men. Grace has anointed your lips, therefore God has blessed you forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Gird your sword on your thigh, mighty one: your splendor and your majesty.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> In your majesty ride on victoriously on behalf of truth, humility, and righteousness. Let your right hand display awesome deeds.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Your arrows are sharp. The nations fall under you, with arrows in the heart of the king's enemies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Your throne, God, is forever and ever. A scepter of equity is the scepter of your kingdom.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> You have loved righteousness, and hated wickedness. Therefore God, your God, has anointed you with the oil of gladness above your fellows.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> All your garments smell like myrrh, aloes, and cassia. Out of ivory palaces stringed instruments have made you glad.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Kings' daughters are among your honorable women. At your right hand the queen stands in gold of Ophir.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Listen, daughter, consider, and turn your ear. Forget your own people, and also your father's house.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> So the king will desire your beauty, honor him, for he is your lord.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> The daughter of Tyre comes with a gift. The rich among the people entreat your favor.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> The princess inside is all glorious. Her clothing is interwoven with gold.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> She shall be led to the king in embroidered work. The virgins, her companions who follow her, shall be brought to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> With gladness and rejoicing they shall be led. They shall enter into the king's palace.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Your sons will take the place of your fathers. You shall make them princes in all the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> I will make your name to be remembered in all generations. Therefore the peoples shall give you thanks forever and ever. {}</div>
</div>
</div>
