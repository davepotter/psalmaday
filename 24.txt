<h3>Psalm 24</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm by David.] The earth is Yahweh's, with its fullness; the world, and those who dwell therein.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> For he has founded it on the seas, and established it on the floods.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Who may ascend to Yahweh's hill? Who may stand in his holy place?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> He who has clean hands and a pure heart; who has not lifted up his soul to falsehood, and has not sworn deceitfully.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> He shall receive a blessing from Yahweh, righteousness from the God of his salvation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> This is the generation of those who seek Him, who seek your face--even Jacob. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Lift up your heads, you gates! Be lifted up, you everlasting doors, and the King of glory will come in.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Who is the King of glory? Yahweh strong and mighty, Yahweh mighty in battle.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Lift up your heads, you gates; yes, lift them up, you everlasting doors, and the King of glory will come in.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Who is this King of glory? Yahweh of Armies is the King of glory! Selah.</div>
</div>
</div>
