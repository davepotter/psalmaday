<h3>Psalm 116</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> I love Yahweh, because he listens to my voice, and my cries for mercy.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Because he has turned his ear to me, therefore I will call on him as long as I live.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> The cords of death surrounded me, the pains of Sheol got a hold of me. I found trouble and sorrow.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Then I called on the name of Yahweh: "Yahweh, I beg you, deliver my soul."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is Gracious and righteous. Yes, our God is merciful.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh preserves the simple. I was brought low, and he saved me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Return to your rest, my soul, for Yahweh has dealt bountifully with you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> For you have delivered my soul from death, my eyes from tears, and my feet from falling.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> I will walk before Yahweh in the land of the living.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> I believed, therefore I said, "I was greatly afflicted."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> I said in my haste, "All men are liars."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> What will I give to Yahweh for all his benefits toward me?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> I will take the cup of salvation, and call on the name of Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> I will pay my vows to Yahweh, yes, in the presence of all his people.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Precious in the sight of Yahweh is the death of his saints.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, truly I am your servant. I am your servant, the son of your handmaid. You have freed me from my chains.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> I will offer to you the sacrifice of thanksgiving, and will call on the name of Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> I will pay my vows to Yahweh, yes, in the presence of all his people,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> in the courts of Yahweh's house, in the midst of you, Jerusalem. Praise Yah!</div>
</div>
</div>
