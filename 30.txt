<h3>Psalm 30</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm. A Song for the Dedication of the Temple. By David.] I will extol you, Yahweh, for you have raised me up, and have not made my foes to rejoice over me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh my God, I cried to you, and you have healed me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, you have brought up my soul from Sheol. You have kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Sing praise to Yahweh, you saints of his. Give thanks to his holy name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For his anger is but for a moment. His favor is for a lifetime. Weeping may stay for the night, but joy comes in the morning.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> As for me, I said in my prosperity, "I shall never be moved."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> You, Yahweh, when you favored me, made my mountain stand strong; but when you hid your face, I was troubled.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> I cried to you, Yahweh. To Yahweh I made supplication:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> "What profit is there in my destruction, if I go down to the pit? Shall the dust praise you? Shall it declare your truth?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Hear, Yahweh, and have mercy on me. Yahweh, be my helper."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> You have turned my mourning into dancing for me. You have removed my sackcloth, and clothed me with gladness,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> To the end that my heart may sing praise to you, and not be silent. Yahweh my God, I will give thanks to you forever!</div>
</div>
</div>
