<h3>Psalm 40</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David.] I waited patiently for Yahweh. He turned to me, and heard my cry.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> He brought me up also out of a horrible pit, out of the miry clay. He set my feet on a rock, and gave me a firm place to stand.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> He has put a new song in my mouth, even praise to our God. Many shall see it, and fear, and shall trust in Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Blessed is the man who makes Yahweh his trust, and doesn't respect the proud, nor such as turn aside to lies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Many, Yahweh, my God, are the wonderful works which you have done, and your thoughts which are toward us. They can't be declared back to you. If I would declare and speak of them, they are more than can be numbered.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Sacrifice and offering you didn't desire. You have opened my ears. You have not required burnt offering and sin offering.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Then I said, "Behold, I have come. It is written about me in the book in the scroll.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> I delight to do your will, my God. Yes, your law is within my heart."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> I have proclaimed glad news of righteousness in the great assembly. Behold, I will not seal my lips, Yahweh, you know.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> I have not hidden your righteousness within my heart. I have declared your faithfulness and your salvation. I have not concealed your loving kindness and your truth from the great assembly.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Don't withhold your tender mercies from me, Yahweh. Let your loving kindness and your truth continually preserve me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> For innumerable evils have surrounded me. My iniquities have overtaken me, so that I am not able to look up. They are more than the hairs of my head. My heart has failed me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Be pleased, Yahweh, to deliver me. Hurry to help me, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Let them be disappointed and confounded together who seek after my soul to destroy it. Let them be turned backward and brought to dishonor who delight in my hurt.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Let them be desolate by reason of their shame that tell me, "Aha! Aha!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Let all those who seek you rejoice and be glad in you. Let such as love your salvation say continually, "Let Yahweh be exalted!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> But I am poor and needy. May the Lord think about me. You are my help and my deliverer. Don't delay, my God.</div>
</div>
</div>
