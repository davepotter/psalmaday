<div class="row">

</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Behold, and answer me, Yahweh, my God. Give light to my eyes, lest I sleep in death;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Lest my enemy say, "I have prevailed against him;" Lest my adversaries rejoice when I fall.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> But I trust in your loving kindness. My heart rejoices in your salvation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> I will sing to Yahweh, because he has been good to me.</div>
</div>
</div>
