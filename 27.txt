<h3>Psalm 27</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David.] Yahweh is my light and my salvation. Whom shall I fear? Yahweh is the strength of my life. Of whom shall I be afraid?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> When evildoers came at me to eat up my flesh, even my adversaries and my foes, they stumbled and fell.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Though an army should encamp against me, my heart shall not fear. Though war should rise against me, even then I will be confident.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> One thing I have asked of Yahweh, that I will seek after, that I may dwell in the house of Yahweh all the days of my life, to see Yahweh's beauty, and to inquire in his temple.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For in the day of trouble he will keep me secretly in his pavilion. In the covert of his tabernacle he will hide me. He will lift me up on a rock.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Now my head will be lifted up above my enemies around me. I will offer sacrifices of joy in his tent. I will sing, yes, I will sing praises to Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Hear, Yahweh, when I cry with my voice. Have mercy also on me, and answer me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> When you said, "Seek my face," my heart said to you, "I will seek your face, Yahweh."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Don't hide your face from me. Don't put your servant away in anger. You have been my help. Don't abandon me, neither forsake me, God of my salvation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> When my father and my mother forsake me, then Yahweh will take me up.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Teach me your way, Yahweh. Lead me in a straight path, because of my enemies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Don't deliver me over to the desire of my adversaries, for false witnesses have risen up against me, such as breathe out cruelty.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> I am still confident of this: I will see the goodness of Yahweh in the land of the living.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Wait for Yahweh. Be strong, and let your heart take courage. Yes, wait for Yahweh.</div>
</div>
</div>
