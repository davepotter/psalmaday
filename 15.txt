<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm by David.] Yahweh, who shall dwell in your sanctuary? Who shall live on your holy hill?</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> He who walks blamelessly does what is right, and speaks truth in his heart;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">

</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> In whose eyes a vile man is despised, but who honors those who fear Yahweh; he who keeps an oath even when it hurts, and doesn't change;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> he who doesn't lend out his money for usury, nor take a bribe against the innocent.  He who does these things shall never be shaken.</div>
