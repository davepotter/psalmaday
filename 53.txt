<h3>Psalm 53</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. To the tune of "Mahalath." A contemplation by David.] The fool has said in his heart, "There is no God." They are corrupt, and have done abominable iniquity. There is no one who does good.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> God looks down from heaven on the children of men, to see if there are any who understood, who seek after God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Every one of them has gone back. They have become filthy together. There is no one who does good, no, not one.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Have the workers of iniquity no knowledge, who eat up my people as they eat bread, and don't call on God?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> There they were in great fear, where no fear was, for God has scattered the bones of him who encamps against you. You have put them to shame, because God has rejected them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Oh that the salvation of Israel would come out of Zion! When God brings back his people from captivity, then Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.</div>
</div>
</div>
