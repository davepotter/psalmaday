<h3>Psalm 129</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Song of Ascents.] Many times they have afflicted me from my youth up. Let Israel now say,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> many times they have afflicted me from my youth up, yet they have not prevailed against me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> The plowers plowed on my back. They made their furrows long.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is righteous. He has cut apart the cords of the wicked.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Let them be disappointed and turned backward, all those who hate Zion.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Let them be as the grass on the housetops, which withers before it grows up;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> with which the reaper doesn't fill his hand, nor he who binds sheaves, his bosom.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Neither do those who go by say, "The blessing of Yahweh be on you. We bless you in the name of Yahweh."</div>
</div>
</div>
