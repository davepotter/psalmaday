<h3>Psalm 90</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Prayer by Moses, the man of God.] Lord, you have been our dwelling place for all generations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Before the mountains were brought forth, before you had formed the earth and the world, even from everlasting to everlasting, you are God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> You turn man to destruction, saying, "Return, you children of men."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> For a thousand years in your sight are just like yesterday when it is past, like a watch in the night.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> You sweep them away as they sleep. In the morning they sprout like new grass.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> In the morning it sprouts and springs up. By evening, it is withered and dry.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> For we are consumed in your anger. We are troubled in your wrath.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> You have set our iniquities before you, our secret sins in the light of your presence.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> For all our days have passed away in your wrath. We bring our years to an end as a sigh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> The days of our years are seventy, or even by reason of strength eighty years; yet their pride is but labor and sorrow, for it passes quickly, and we fly away.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Who knows the power of your anger, your wrath according to the fear that is due to you?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> So teach us to number our days, that we may gain a heart of wisdom.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Relent, Yahweh! How long? Have compassion on your servants!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Satisfy us in the morning with your loving kindness, that we may rejoice and be glad all our days.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Make us glad for as many days as you have afflicted us, for as many years as we have seen evil.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Let your work appear to your servants; your glory to their children.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Let the favor of the Lord our God be on us; establish the work of our hands for us; yes, establish the work of our hands.</div>
</div>
</div>
