<h3>Psalm 96</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Sing to Yahweh a new song! Sing to Yahweh, all the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Sing to Yahweh! Bless his name! Proclaim his salvation from day to day!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Declare his glory among the nations, his marvelous works among all the peoples.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> For great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised! He is to be feared above all gods.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For all the gods of the peoples are idols, but Yahweh made the heavens.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Honor and majesty are before him. Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Ascribe to Yahweh, you families of nations, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name. Bring an offering, and come into his courts.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Worship Yahweh in holy array. Tremble before him, all the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Say among the nations, "Yahweh reigns." The world is also established. It can't be moved. He will judge the peoples with equity.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice. Let the sea roar, and its fullness!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Let the field and all that is in it exult! Then all the trees of the woods shall sing for joy</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> before Yahweh; for he comes, for he comes to judge the earth. He will judge the world with righteousness, the peoples with his truth.</div>
</div>
</div>
