<h3>Psalm 101</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Psalm by David.] I will sing of loving kindness and justice. To you, Yahweh, I will sing praises.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> I will be careful to live a blameless life. When will you come to me? I will walk within my house with a blameless heart.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> I will set no vile thing before my eyes. I hate the deeds of faithless men. They will not cling to me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> A perverse heart will be far from me. I will have nothing to do with evil.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> I will silence whoever secretly slanders his neighbor. I won't tolerate one who is haughty and conceited.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> My eyes will be on the faithful of the land, that they may dwell with me. He who walks in a perfect way, he will serve me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> He who practices deceit won't dwell within my house. He who speaks falsehood won't be established before my eyes.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Morning by morning, I will destroy all the wicked of the land; to cut off all the workers of iniquity from Yahweh's city.</div>
</div>
</div>
