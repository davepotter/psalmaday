<h3>Psalm 83</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A song. A Psalm by Asaph.] God, don't keep silent. Don't keep silent, and don't be still, God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> For, behold, your enemies are stirred up. Those who hate you have lifted up their heads.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> They conspire with cunning against your people. They plot against your cherished ones.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> "Come," they say, "and let's destroy them as a nation, that the name of Israel may be remembered no more."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For they have conspired together with one mind. They form an alliance against you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> The tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites; Moab, and the Hagrites;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek; Philistia with the inhabitants of Tyre;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Assyria also is joined with them. They have helped the children of Lot. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Do to them as you did to Midian, as to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the river Kishon;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> who perished at Endor, who became as dung for the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb; yes, all their princes like Zebah and Zalmunna;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> who said, "Let us take possession of God's pasturelands."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> My God, make them like tumbleweed; like chaff before the wind.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> As the fire that burns the forest, as the flame that sets the mountains on fire,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> so pursue them with your tempest, and terrify them with your storm.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Fill their faces with confusion, that they may seek your name, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Let them be disappointed and dismayed forever. Yes, let them be confounded and perish;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> that they may know that you alone, whose name is Yahweh, are the Most High over all the earth.</div>
</div>
</div>
