<h3>Psalm 74</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A contemplation by Asaph.] God, why have you rejected us forever? Why does your anger smolder against the sheep of your pasture?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Remember your congregation, which you purchased of old, which you have redeemed to be the tribe of your inheritance; Mount Zion, in which you have lived.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Lift up your feet to the perpetual ruins, all the evil that the enemy has done in the sanctuary.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Your adversaries have roared in the midst of your assembly. They have set up their standards as signs.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> They behaved like men wielding axes, cutting through a thicket of trees.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Now they break all its carved work down with hatchet and hammers.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> They have burned your sanctuary to the ground. They have profaned the dwelling place of your Name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> They said in their heart, "We will crush them completely." They have burned up all the places in the land where God was worshiped.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> We see no miraculous signs. There is no longer any prophet, neither is there among us anyone who knows how long.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> How long, God, shall the adversary reproach? Shall the enemy blaspheme your name forever?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Why do you draw back your hand, even your right hand? Take it out of your pocket and consume them!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Yet God is my King of old, working salvation in the midst of the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> You divided the sea by your strength. You broke the heads of the sea monsters in the waters.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> You broke the heads of Leviathan in pieces. You gave him as food to people and desert creatures.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> You opened up spring and stream. You dried up mighty rivers.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> The day is yours, the night is also yours. You have prepared the light and the sun.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> You have set all the boundaries of the earth. You have made summer and winter.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Remember this, that the enemy has mocked you, Yahweh. Foolish people have blasphemed your name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Don't deliver the soul of your dove to wild beasts. Don't forget the life of your poor forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> Honor your covenant, for haunts of violence fill the dark places of the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Don't let the oppressed return ashamed. Let the poor and needy praise your name.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Arise, God! Plead your own cause. Remember how the foolish man mocks you all day.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Don't forget the voice of your adversaries. The tumult of those who rise up against you ascends continually.</div>
</div>
</div>
