<h3>Psalm 7</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A meditation by David, which he sang to Yahweh, concerning the words of Cush, the Benjamite.] Yahweh, my God, I take refuge in you. Save me from all those who pursue me, and deliver me,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> lest they tear apart my soul like a lion, ripping it in pieces, while there is none to deliver.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, my God, if I have done this, if there is iniquity in my hands,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> if I have rewarded evil to him who was at peace with me (yes, if I have delivered him who without cause was my adversary),</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> let the enemy pursue my soul, and overtake it; yes, let him tread my life down to the earth, and lay my glory in the dust. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Arise, Yahweh, in your anger. Lift up yourself against the rage of my adversaries. Awake for me. You have commanded judgment.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Let the congregation of the peoples surround you. Rule over them on high.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh administers judgment to the peoples. Judge me, Yahweh, according to my righteousness, and to my integrity that is in me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Oh let the wickedness of the wicked come to an end, but establish the righteous; their minds and hearts are searched by the righteous God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> My shield is with God, who saves the upright in heart.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> God is a righteous judge, yes, a God who has indignation every day.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> If a man doesn't relent, he will sharpen his sword; he has bent and strung his bow.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> He has also prepared for himself the instruments of death. He makes ready his flaming arrows.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Behold, he travails with iniquity. Yes, he has conceived mischief, and brought forth falsehood.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> He has dug a hole, and has fallen into the pit which he made.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> The trouble he causes shall return to his own head. His violence shall come down on the crown of his own head.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> I will give thanks to Yahweh according to his righteousness, and will sing praise to the name of Yahweh Most High.</div>
</div>
</div>
