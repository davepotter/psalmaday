<h3>Psalm 51</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David, when Nathan the prophet came to him, after he had gone in to Bathsheba.] Have mercy on me, God, according to your loving kindness. According to the multitude of your tender mercies, blot out my transgressions.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Wash me thoroughly from my iniquity. Cleanse me from my sin.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For I know my transgressions. My sin is constantly before me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Against you, and you only, have I sinned, and done that which is evil in your sight; that you may be proved right when you speak, and justified when you judge.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Behold, I was brought forth in iniquity. In sin my mother conceived me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Behold, you desire truth in the inward parts. You teach me wisdom in the inmost place.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Purify me with hyssop, and I will be clean. Wash me, and I will be whiter than snow.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Let me hear joy and gladness, That the bones which you have broken may rejoice.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Hide your face from my sins, and blot out all of my iniquities.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Create in me a clean heart, O God. Renew a right spirit within me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Don't throw me from your presence, and don't take your holy Spirit from me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Restore to me the joy of your salvation. Uphold me with a willing spirit.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Then I will teach transgressors your ways. Sinners shall be converted to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, the God of my salvation. My tongue shall sing aloud of your righteousness.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Lord, open my lips. My mouth shall declare your praise.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> For you don't delight in sacrifice, or else I would give it. You have no pleasure in burnt offering.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit. A broken and contrite heart, O God, you will not despise.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Do well in your good pleasure to Zion. Build the walls of Jerusalem.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Then you will delight in the sacrifices of righteousness, in burnt offerings and in whole burnt offerings. Then they will offer bulls on your altar.</div>
</div>
</div>
