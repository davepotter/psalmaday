<h3>Psalm 10</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Why do you stand far off, Yahweh? Why do you hide yourself in times of trouble?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> In arrogance, the wicked hunt down the weak. They are caught in the schemes that they devise.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For the wicked boasts of his heart's cravings. He blesses the greedy, and condemns Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> The wicked, in the pride of his face, has no room in his thoughts for God.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> His ways are prosperous at all times. He is haughty, and your laws are far from his sight. As for all his adversaries, he sneers at them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> He says in his heart, "I shall not be shaken. For generations I shall have no trouble."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> His mouth is full of cursing, deceit, and oppression. Under his tongue is mischief and iniquity.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> He lies in wait near the villages. From ambushes, he murders the innocent. His eyes are secretly set against the helpless.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> He lurks in secret as a lion in his ambush. He lies in wait to catch the helpless. He catches the helpless, when he draws him in his net.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> The helpless are crushed. They collapse. They fall under his strength.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> He says in his heart, "God has forgotten. He hides his face. He will never see it."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Arise, Yahweh! God, lift up your hand! Don't forget the helpless.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Why does the wicked person condemn God, and say in his heart, "God won't call me into account?"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> But you do see trouble and grief. You consider it to take it into your hand. You help the victim and the fatherless.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Break the arm of the wicked. As for the evil man, seek out his wickedness until you find none.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is King forever and ever! The nations will perish out of his land.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, you have heard the desire of the humble. You will prepare their heart. You will cause your ear to hear,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> to judge the fatherless and the oppressed, that man who is of the earth may terrify no more.</div>
</div>
</div>
