<h3>Psalm 17</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Prayer by David.] Hear, Yahweh, my righteous plea; Give ear to my prayer, that doesn't go out of deceitful lips.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Let my sentence come forth from your presence. Let your eyes look on equity.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> You have proved my heart. You have visited me in the night. You have tried me, and found nothing. I have resolved that my mouth shall not disobey.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> As for the works of men, by the word of your lips, I have kept myself from the ways of the violent.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> My steps have held fast to your paths. My feet have not slipped.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> I have called on you, for you will answer me, God. Turn your ear to me. Hear my speech.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Show your marvelous loving kindness, you who save those who take refuge by your right hand from their enemies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Keep me as the apple of your eye. Hide me under the shadow of your wings,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> from the wicked who oppress me, my deadly enemies, who surround me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> They close up their callous hearts. With their mouth they speak proudly.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> They have now surrounded us in our steps. They set their eyes to cast us down to the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> He is like a lion that is greedy of his prey, as it were a young lion lurking in secret places.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Arise, Yahweh, confront him. Cast him down. Deliver my soul from the wicked by your sword;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> from men by your hand, Yahweh, from men of the world, whose portion is in this life. You fill the belly of your cherished ones. Your sons have plenty, and they store up wealth for their children.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> As for me, I shall see your face in righteousness. I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with seeing your form.</div>
</div>
</div>
