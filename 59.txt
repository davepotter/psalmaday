<h3>Psalm 59</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. To the tune of "Do Not Destroy." A poem by David, when Saul sent, and they watched the house to kill him.] Deliver me from my enemies, my God. Set me on high from those who rise up against me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Deliver me from the workers of iniquity. Save me from the bloodthirsty men.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> For, behold, they lie in wait for my soul. The mighty gather themselves together against me, not for my disobedience, nor for my sin, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> I have done no wrong, yet they are ready to attack me. Rise up, behold, and help me!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> You, Yahweh God of Armies, the God of Israel, rouse yourself to punish the nations. Show no mercy to the wicked traitors. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> They return at evening, howling like dogs, and prowl around the city.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Behold, they spew with their mouth. Swords are in their lips, "For," they say, "who hears us?"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> But you, Yahweh, laugh at them. You scoff at all the nations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Oh, my Strength, I watch for you, for God is my high tower.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> My God will go before me with his loving kindness. God will let me look at my enemies in triumph.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Don't kill them, or my people may forget. Scatter them by your power, and bring them down, Lord our shield.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> For the sin of their mouth, and the words of their lips, let them be caught in their pride, for the curses and lies which they utter.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Consume them in wrath. Consume them, and they will be no more. Let them know that God rules in Jacob, to the ends of the earth. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> At evening let them return. Let them howl like a dog, and go around the city.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> They shall wander up and down for food, and wait all night if they aren't satisfied.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> But I will sing of your strength. Yes, I will sing aloud of your loving kindness in the morning. For you have been my high tower, a refuge in the day of my distress.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> To you, my strength, I will sing praises. For God is my high tower, the God of my mercy.</div>
</div>
</div>
