<h3>Psalm 71</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> In you, Yahweh, I take refuge. Never let me be disappointed.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Deliver me in your righteousness, and rescue me. Turn your ear to me, and save me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Be to me a rock of refuge to which I may always go. Give the command to save me, for you are my rock and my fortress.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Rescue me, my God, from the hand of the wicked, from the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For you are my hope, Lord Yahweh; my confidence from my youth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> I have relied on you from the womb. You are he who took me out of my mother's womb. I will always praise you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> I am a marvel to many, but you are my strong refuge.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> My mouth shall be filled with your praise, with your honor all the day.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Don't reject me in my old age. Don't forsake me when my strength fails.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> For my enemies talk about me. Those who watch for my soul conspire together,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> saying, "God has forsaken him. Pursue and take him, for no one will rescue him."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> God, don't be far from me. My God, hurry to help me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Let my accusers be disappointed and consumed. Let them be covered with disgrace and scorn who want to harm me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> But I will always hope, and will add to all of your praise.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> My mouth will tell about your righteousness, and of your salvation all day, though I don't know its full measure.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> I will come with the mighty acts of the Lord Yahweh. I will make mention of your righteousness, even of yours alone.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> God, you have taught me from my youth. Until now, I have declared your wondrous works.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Yes, even when I am old and gray-haired, God, don't forsake me, until I have declared your strength to the next generation, your might to everyone who is to come.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Your righteousness also, God, reaches to the heavens; you have done great things. God, who is like you?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> You, who have shown us many and bitter troubles, you will let me live. You will bring us up again from the depths of the earth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Increase my honor, and comfort me again.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> I will also praise you with the harp for your faithfulness, my God. I sing praises to you with the lyre, Holy One of Israel.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> My lips shall shout for joy! My soul, which you have redeemed, sings praises to you!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> My tongue will also talk about your righteousness all day long, for they are disappointed, and they are confounded, who want to harm me.</div>
</div>
</div>
