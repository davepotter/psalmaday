<h3>Psalm 70</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. By David. A reminder.] Hurry, God, to deliver me. Come quickly to help me, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Let them be disappointed and confounded who seek my soul. Let those who desire my ruin be turned back in disgrace.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Let them be turned because of their shame Who say, "Aha! Aha!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Let all those who seek you rejoice and be glad in you. Let those who love your salvation continually say, "Let God be exalted!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> But I am poor and needy. Come to me quickly, God. You are my help and my deliverer. Yahweh, don't delay.</div>
</div>
</div>
