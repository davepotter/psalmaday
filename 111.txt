<h3>Psalm 111</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yah!{Psalm 111 is an acrostic poem, with each verse after the initial "Praise Yah!" starting with a letter of the alphabet (ordered from Alef to Tav).} I will give thanks to Yahweh with my whole heart, in the council of the upright, and in the congregation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh's works are great, pondered by all those who delight in them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> His work is honor and majesty. His righteousness endures forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> He has caused his wonderful works to be remembered. Yahweh is gracious and merciful.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> He has given food to those who fear him. He always remembers his covenant.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> He has shown his people the power of his works, in giving them the heritage of the nations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> The works of his hands are truth and justice. All his precepts are sure.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> They are established forever and ever. They are done in truth and uprightness.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> He has sent redemption to his people. He has ordained his covenant forever. His name is holy and awesome!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom. All those who do his work have a good understanding. His praise endures forever!</div>
</div>
</div>
