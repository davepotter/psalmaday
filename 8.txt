<h3>Psalm 8</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician; on an instrument of Gath. A Psalm by David.] Yahweh, our Lord, how majestic is your name in all the earth, who has set your glory above the heavens!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> From the lips of babes and infants you have established strength, because of your adversaries, that you might silence the enemy and the avenger.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> When I consider your heavens, the work of your fingers, the moon and the stars, which you have ordained;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> what is man, that you think of him? What is the son of man, that you care for him?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> For you have made him a little lower than God,{Hebrew: Elohim. The word Elohim, used here, usually means "God," but can also mean "gods," "princes," or "angels."} and crowned him with glory and honor.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> You make him ruler over the works of your hands. You have put all things under his feet:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> All sheep and cattle, yes, and the animals of the field,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> The birds of the sky, the fish of the sea, and whatever passes through the paths of the seas.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, our Lord, how majestic is your name in all the earth!</div>
</div>
</div>
