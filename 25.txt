<h3>Psalm 25</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David.] To you, Yahweh, do I lift up my soul.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> My God, I have trusted in you. Don't let me be shamed. Don't let my enemies triumph over me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Yes, no one who waits for you shall be shamed. They shall be shamed who deal treacherously without cause.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Show me your ways, Yahweh. Teach me your paths.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Guide me in your truth, and teach me, For you are the God of my salvation, I wait for you all day long.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, remember your tender mercies and your loving kindness, for they are from old times.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Don't remember the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions. Remember me according to your loving kindness, for your goodness' sake, Yahweh.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Good and upright is Yahweh, therefore he will instruct sinners in the way.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> He will guide the humble in justice. He will teach the humble his way.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> All the paths of Yahweh are loving kindness and truth to such as keep his covenant and his testimonies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> For your name's sake, Yahweh, pardon my iniquity, for it is great.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> What man is he who fears Yahweh? He shall instruct him in the way that he shall choose.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> His soul shall dwell at ease. His seed shall inherit the land.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> The friendship of Yahweh is with those who fear him. He will show them his covenant.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> My eyes are ever on Yahweh, for he will pluck my feet out of the net.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Turn to me, and have mercy on me, for I am desolate and afflicted.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> The troubles of my heart are enlarged. Oh bring me out of my distresses.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Consider my affliction and my travail. Forgive all my sins.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Consider my enemies, for they are many. They hate me with cruel hatred.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> Oh keep my soul, and deliver me. Let me not be disappointed, for I take refuge in you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, for I wait for you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Redeem Israel, God, out all of his troubles.</div>
</div>
</div>
