<h3>Psalm 103</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David.] Praise Yahweh, my soul! All that is within me, praise his holy name!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yahweh, my soul, and don't forget all his benefits;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> who forgives all your sins; who heals all your diseases;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> who redeems your life from destruction; who crowns you with loving kindness and tender mercies;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> who satisfies your desire with good things, so that your youth is renewed like the eagle's.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh executes righteous acts, and justice for all who are oppressed.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> He made known his ways to Moses, his deeds to the children of Israel.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh is merciful and gracious, slow to anger, and abundant in loving kindness.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> He will not always accuse; neither will he stay angry forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> He has not dealt with us according to our sins, nor repaid us for our iniquities.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> For as the heavens are high above the earth, so great is his loving kindness toward those who fear him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> As far as the east is from the west, so far has he removed our transgressions from us.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Like a father has compassion on his children, so Yahweh has compassion on those who fear him.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> For he knows how we are made. He remembers that we are dust.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> As for man, his days are like grass. As a flower of the field, so he flourishes.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> For the wind passes over it, and it is gone. Its place remembers it no more.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> But Yahweh's loving kindness is from everlasting to everlasting with those who fear him, his righteousness to children's children;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> to those who keep his covenant, to those who remember to obey his precepts.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh has established his throne in the heavens. His kingdom rules over all.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yahweh, you angels of his, who are mighty in strength, who fulfill his word, obeying the voice of his word.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yahweh, all you armies of his, you servants of his, who do his pleasure.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Praise Yahweh, all you works of his, in all places of his dominion. Praise Yahweh, my soul!</div>
</div>
</div>
