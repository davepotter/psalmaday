<h3>Psalm 57</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. To the tune of "Do Not Destroy." A poem by David, when he fled from Saul, in the cave.] Be merciful to me, God, be merciful to me, for my soul takes refuge in you. Yes, in the shadow of your wings, I will take refuge, until disaster has passed.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> I cry out to God Most High, to God who accomplishes my requests for me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> He will send from heaven, and save me, he rebukes the one who is pursuing me. Selah. God will send out his loving kindness and his truth.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> My soul is among lions. I lie among those who are set on fire, even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows, and their tongue a sharp sword.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Be exalted, God, above the heavens! Let your glory be above all the earth!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> They have prepared a net for my steps. My soul is bowed down. They dig a pit before me. They fall into its midst themselves. Selah.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> My heart is steadfast, God, my heart is steadfast. I will sing, yes, I will sing praises.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Wake up, my glory! Wake up, psaltery and harp! I will wake up the dawn.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> I will give thanks to you, Lord, among the peoples. I will sing praises to you among the nations.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> For your great loving kindness reaches to the heavens, and your truth to the skies.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Be exalted, God, above the heavens. Let your glory be over all the earth.</div>
</div>
</div>
