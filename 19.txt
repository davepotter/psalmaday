<h3>Psalm 19</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by David.] The heavens declare the glory of God. The expanse shows his handiwork.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Day after day they pour forth speech, and night after night they display knowledge.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Their voice has gone out through all the earth, their words to the end of the world. In them he has set a tent for the sun,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber, like a strong man rejoicing to run his course.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> His going forth is from the end of the heavens, his circuit to its ends; There is nothing hidden from its heat.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh's law is perfect, restoring the soul. Yahweh's testimony is sure, making wise the simple.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh's precepts are right, rejoicing the heart. Yahweh's commandment is pure, enlightening the eyes.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> The fear of Yahweh is clean, enduring forever. Yahweh's ordinances are true, and righteous altogether.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> More to be desired are they than gold, yes, than much fine gold; sweeter also than honey and the extract of the honeycomb.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Moreover by them is your servant warned. In keeping them there is great reward.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Who can discern his errors? Forgive me from hidden errors.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Keep back your servant also from presumptuous sins. Let them not have dominion over me. Then I will be upright. I will be blameless and innocent of great transgression.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be acceptable in your sight, Yahweh, my rock, and my redeemer.</div>
</div>
</div>
