<h3>Psalm 144</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David.] Blessed be Yahweh, my rock, who teaches my hands to war, and my fingers to battle:</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> my loving kindness, my fortress, my high tower, my deliverer, my shield, and he in whom I take refuge; who subdues my people under me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, what is man, that you care for him? Or the son of man, that you think of him?</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Man is like a breath. His days are like a shadow that passes away.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Part your heavens, Yahweh, and come down. Touch the mountains, and they will smoke.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Throw out lightning, and scatter them. Send out your arrows, and rout them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> Stretch out your hand from above, rescue me, and deliver me out of great waters, out of the hands of foreigners;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> whose mouths speak deceit, Whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> I will sing a new song to you, God. On a ten-stringed lyre, I will sing praises to you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> You are he who gives salvation to kings, who rescues David, his servant, from the deadly sword.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Rescue me, and deliver me out of the hands of foreigners, whose mouths speak deceit, whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Then our sons will be like well-nurtured plants, our daughters like pillars carved to adorn a palace.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Our barns are full, filled with all kinds of provision. Our sheep bring forth thousands and ten thousands in our fields.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> Our oxen will pull heavy loads. There is no breaking in, and no going away, and no outcry in our streets.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Happy are the people who are in such a situation. Happy are the people whose God is Yahweh. {}</div>
</div>
