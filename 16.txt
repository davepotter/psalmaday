<h3>Psalm 16</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [A Poem by David.] Preserve me, God, for in you do I take refuge.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> My soul, you have said to Yahweh, "You are my Lord. Apart from you I have no good thing."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> As for the saints who are in the earth, they are the excellent ones in whom is all my delight.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Their sorrows shall be multiplied who give gifts to another god. Their drink offerings of blood I will not offer, nor take their names on my lips.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh assigned my portion and my cup. You made my lot secure.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places. Yes, I have a good inheritance.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> I will bless Yahweh, who has given me counsel. Yes, my heart instructs me in the night seasons.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> I have set Yahweh always before me. Because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> Therefore my heart is glad, and my tongue rejoices. My body shall also dwell in safety.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> For you will not leave my soul in Sheol, neither will you allow your holy one to see corruption.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> You will show me the path of life. In your presence is fullness of joy. In your right hand there are pleasures forevermore.</div>
</div>
</div>
