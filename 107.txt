<h3>Psalm 107</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good, for his loving kindness endures forever.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Let the redeemed by Yahweh say so, whom he has redeemed from the hand of the adversary,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> And gathered out of the lands, from the east and from the west, from the north and from the south.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> They wandered in the wilderness in a desert way. They found no city to live in.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Hungry and thirsty, their soul fainted in them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Then they cried to Yahweh in their trouble, and he delivered them out of their distresses,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> he led them also by a straight way, that they might go to a city to live in.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works to the children of men!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> For he satisfies the longing soul. He fills the hungry soul with good.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Some sat in darkness and in the shadow of death, being bound in affliction and iron,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> because they rebelled against the words of God, and condemned the counsel of the Most High.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> Therefore he brought down their heart with labor. They fell down, and there was none to help.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> Then they cried to Yahweh in their trouble, and he saved them out of their distresses.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> He brought them out of darkness and the shadow of death, and broke their bonds in sunder.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works to the children of men!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> For he has broken the gates of brass, and cut through bars of iron.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Fools are afflicted because of their disobedience, and because of their iniquities.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> Their soul abhors all kinds of food. They draw near to the gates of death.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Then they cry to Yahweh in their trouble, he saves them out of their distresses.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> He sends his word, and heals them, and delivers them from their graves.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works to the children of men!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> Let them offer the sacrifices of thanksgiving, and declare his works with singing.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Those who go down to the sea in ships, who do business in great waters;</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> These see Yahweh's works, and his wonders in the deep.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> For he commands, and raises the stormy wind, which lifts up its waves.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> They mount up to the sky; they go down again to the depths. Their soul melts away because of trouble.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">27</div><div class="verse"> They reel back and forth, and stagger like a drunken man, and are at their wits' end.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">28</div><div class="verse"> Then they cry to Yahweh in their trouble, and he brings them out of their distress.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">29</div><div class="verse"> He makes the storm a calm, so that its waves are still.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">30</div><div class="verse"> Then they are glad because it is calm, so he brings them to their desired haven.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">31</div><div class="verse"> Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works for the children of men!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">32</div><div class="verse"> Let them exalt him also in the assembly of the people, and praise him in the seat of the elders.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">33</div><div class="verse"> He turns rivers into a desert, water springs into a thirsty ground,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">34</div><div class="verse"> and a fruitful land into a salt waste, for the wickedness of those who dwell in it.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">35</div><div class="verse"> He turns a desert into a pool of water, and a dry land into water springs.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">36</div><div class="verse"> There he makes the hungry live, that they may prepare a city to live in,</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">37</div><div class="verse"> sow fields, plant vineyards, and reap the fruits of increase.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">38</div><div class="verse"> He blesses them also, so that they are multiplied greatly. He doesn't allow their livestock to decrease.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">39</div><div class="verse"> Again, they are diminished and bowed down through oppression, trouble, and sorrow.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">40</div><div class="verse"> He pours contempt on princes, and causes them to wander in a trackless waste.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">41</div><div class="verse"> Yet he lifts the needy out of their affliction, and increases their families like a flock.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">42</div><div class="verse"> The upright will see it, and be glad. All the wicked will shut their mouths.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">43</div><div class="verse"> Whoever is wise will pay attention to these things. They will consider the loving kindnesses of Yahweh.</div>
</div>
</div>
