<h3>Psalm 5</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [For the Chief Musician, with the flutes. A Psalm by David.] Give ear to my words, Yahweh. Consider my meditation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Listen to the voice of my cry, my King and my God; for to you do I pray.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Yahweh, in the morning you shall hear my voice. In the morning I will lay my requests before you, and will watch expectantly.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> For you are not a God who has pleasure in wickedness. Evil can't live with you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> The arrogant shall not stand in your sight. You hate all workers of iniquity.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> You will destroy those who speak lies. Yahweh abhors the bloodthirsty and deceitful man.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> But as for me, in the abundance of your loving kindness I will come into your house. I will bow toward your holy temple in reverence of you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Lead me, Yahweh, in your righteousness because of my enemies. Make your way straight before my face.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> For there is no faithfulness in their mouth. Their heart is destruction. Their throat is an open tomb. They flatter with their tongue.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> Hold them guilty, God. Let them fall by their own counsels; Thrust them out in the multitude of their transgressions, for they have rebelled against you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> But let all those who take refuge in you rejoice, Let them always shout for joy, because you defend them. Let them also who love your name be joyful in you.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> For you will bless the righteous. Yahweh, you will surround him with favor as with a shield.</div>
</div>
</div>
