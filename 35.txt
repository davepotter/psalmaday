<h3>Psalm 35</h3>
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="number">1</div><div class="verse"> [By David.] Contend, Yahweh, with those who contend with me. Fight against those who fight against me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">2</div><div class="verse"> Take hold of shield and buckler, and stand up for my help.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">3</div><div class="verse"> Brandish the spear and block those who pursue me. Tell my soul, "I am your salvation."</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">4</div><div class="verse"> Let those who seek after my soul be disappointed and brought to dishonor. Let those who plot my ruin be turned back and confounded.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">5</div><div class="verse"> Let them be as chaff before the wind, Yahweh's angel driving them on.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">6</div><div class="verse"> Let their way be dark and slippery, Yahweh's angel pursuing them.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">7</div><div class="verse"> For without cause they have hidden their net in a pit for me. Without cause they have dug a pit for my soul.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">8</div><div class="verse"> Let destruction come on him unawares. Let his net that he has hidden catch himself. Let him fall into that destruction.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">9</div><div class="verse"> My soul shall be joyful in Yahweh. It shall rejoice in his salvation.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">10</div><div class="verse"> All my bones shall say, "Yahweh, who is like you, who delivers the poor from him who is too strong for him; yes, the poor and the needy from him who robs him?"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">11</div><div class="verse"> Unrighteous witnesses rise up. They ask me about things that I don't know about.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">12</div><div class="verse"> They reward me evil for good, to the bereaving of my soul.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">13</div><div class="verse"> But as for me, when they were sick, my clothing was sackcloth. I afflicted my soul with fasting. My prayer returned into my own bosom.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">14</div><div class="verse"> I behaved myself as though it had been my friend or my brother. I bowed down mourning, as one who mourns his mother.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">15</div><div class="verse"> But in my adversity, they rejoiced, and gathered themselves together. The attackers gathered themselves together against me, and I didn't know it. They tore at me, and didn't cease.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">16</div><div class="verse"> Like the profane mockers in feasts, they gnashed their teeth at me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">17</div><div class="verse"> Lord, how long will you look on? Rescue my soul from their destruction, my precious life from the lions.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">18</div><div class="verse"> I will give you thanks in the great assembly. I will praise you among many people.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">19</div><div class="verse"> Don't let those who are my enemies wrongfully rejoice over me; neither let those who hate me without a cause wink their eyes.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">20</div><div class="verse"> For they don't speak peace, but they devise deceitful words against those who are quiet in the land.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">21</div><div class="verse"> Yes, they opened their mouth wide against me. They said, "Aha! Aha! Our eye has seen it!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">22</div><div class="verse"> You have seen it, Yahweh. Don't keep silent. Lord, don't be far from me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">23</div><div class="verse"> Wake up! Rise up to defend me, my God! My Lord, contend for me!</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">24</div><div class="verse"> Vindicate me, Yahweh my God, according to your righteousness. Don't let them gloat over me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">25</div><div class="verse"> Don't let them say in their heart, "Aha! That's the way we want it!" Don't let them say, "We have swallowed him up!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">26</div><div class="verse"> Let them be disappointed and confounded together who rejoice at my calamity. Let them be clothed with shame and dishonor who magnify themselves against me.</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">27</div><div class="verse"> Let them shout for joy and be glad, who favor my righteous cause. Yes, let them say continually, "Yahweh be magnified, who has pleasure in the prosperity of his servant!"</div>
</div>
<div class="row">
<div class="number">28</div><div class="verse"> My tongue shall talk about your righteousness and about your praise all day long.</div>
</div>
</div>
